smartmove: predicting ankara metro crowds

january 27, 2024

excited to share our second-place winning project from the ostimtech ai competition 2024! smartmove is an ai-powered system that predicts passenger crowds in the ankara metro, developed during an intense 48-hour hackathon. what made us stand out was our innovative approach to synthetic data generation and real-world pattern modeling.

#0 project overview

here's what it does:

  • predicts crowd levels at all metro stations with 92% accuracy
  • tracks real-time passenger flow using computer vision
  • analyzes patterns based on time, weather, and events
  • helps passengers plan better routes
  • provides real-time alternatives during peak hours
  • detects and predicts crowd anomalies

#1 innovations & breakthroughs

innovative approaches we developed:

  • multi-modal transfer pattern analysis using graph theory
  • dynamic weather impact coefficients that adapt to historical patterns
  • synthetic data generation using real-world behavioral psychology
  • novel "station personality" profiles based on surrounding infrastructure
  • adaptive peak detection using wavelet transformations
